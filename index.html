HELLO FROM W8MB!

My amateur radio journey began in 2020. It started with off-roading and a YouTube video about trail communications that introduced me to ham radio. What began as a way to talk on the trail has grown into a deep passion for learning, building, experimenting, and helping others discover the hobby.

Today, I love operating HF portable, assembling antennas, digital modes, and diving head-first into Morse code. I also serve as an EMT and volunteer firefighter, and enjoy keeping our department's radio programming optimized. Thanks for stopping by!
