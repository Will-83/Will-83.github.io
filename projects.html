Project: Budget Portable Wire Dipoles

A rugged, lightweight, and highly packable dipole designed specifically for POTA/SOTA operations.

Step 1

The Center Feedpoint

The heart of any portable dipole is its center connection. It needs to handle tension while keeping water out of the coax junction.

  • Strain Relief: Wire connections are mechanically secured using high-strength 3D printed PETG center pieces.
  • Weatherproofing: Junctions are sealed with marine-grade heat shrink tubing to prevent moisture ingress.
  • Connection: Terminated with a standard BNC connector for quick deployment in the field.
Step 2

Wire Elements & Winders

For portable work, wire memory is the enemy. The elements need to deploy easily without turning into a bird's nest.

  • The Wire: Built using ultra-flexible, lightweight 18 AWG copper-clad steel to ensure high conductivity and zero kinking.
  • Tensioners: Lightweight end-insulators allow easy attachment of throw-lines.
  • Storage: Winders are custom printed in bright PETG for high visibility in the grass so nothing gets left behind at the activation site.
